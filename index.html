“MUN Impact is a global community that believes in the power of Model United Nations to inform, inspire, and motivate its participants to action.” An non-profit organization, MUN Impact is dedicated to the vision of bringing Model UN conferences around the world to promote the objectives of the UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). MUN Impact aims to develop a global platform to promote the UN SDGs through ways such as but not limited to cooperating with MUN conferences, making the public more aware of the SDGs and its related objectives, as well as mentioning key MUN programs the organization deems would advance their vision in a positive direction.

Here at AIMUN, we believe the power of the individual is great, but we also believe that the power of cooperation extends beyond imagination. With the similarity between the visions of AIMUN and MUN Impact, we believe that having this organization as a partner, a collaborator, and a sponsor would further advance our global vision for Model United Nations and simultaneously play a key role in MUN Impact’s mission to promote the UN Sustainable Development Goals.

Please visit MUN Impact’s website for MORE information: http://munimpact.org/
